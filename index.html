Project Euler 025

1000-Digit Fibonacci Number

The Fibonacci Sequence is defined by the recurrence relation:

Fn = Fn-1 + Fn-2, where F1 = 1 and F2 = 1.

Hence the first 12 terms will be:


F1 = 1
F2 = 1
F3 = 2
F4 = 3
F5 = 5
F6 = 8
F7 = 13
F8 = 21
F9 = 34
F10 = 55
F11 = 89
F12 = 144

Find the index of the first term in the Fibonacci sequence to contain n digits. Begin by entering the number of digits.
